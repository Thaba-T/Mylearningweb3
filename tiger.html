TIGER

The tiger is a symbol of raw power and untamed beauty. As the largest of the big cats, it dominates its territory with unmatched strength and stealth. Its fiery orange coat and bold black stripes make it one of nature’s most striking predators. Fiercely independent and highly intelligent, the tiger is an apex predator, built for speed, agility, and deadly precision. No wonder it’s revered as the king of the jungle. 🐅
